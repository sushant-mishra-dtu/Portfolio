Subject profile

Who is behind the bench, in plain terms.

I'm an electrical engineering student at Delhi Technological University, on a B.Tech that runs from 2025 to 2029. I work at the layer where hardware meets intelligence: RTL design on FPGAs, machine learning small enough to live on a microcontroller, and the timing budgets that decide whether an idea survives contact with real silicon.

Most of what I build starts on a bench, not a cloud console. The FPGA accelerator that anchors this site runs per-frame anomaly detection in 57.17 microseconds with no network in the loop. I pay attention to the nanoseconds because on hardware they are the difference between a system that ships and one that misses its deadline.

Away from the lab I have shipped full-stack web apps in production during an internship, and led a hackathon team through a mid-event pivot. Right now I am open to internships and hardware or edge-AI roles.
